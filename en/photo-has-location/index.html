Check whether a photo has GPS location

Get a simple, understandable answer: your photo either does or does not contain GPS location data.

  • Highly visible Yes or No result.
  • Explanation for non-technical users.
  • Direct link to the tool that removes location.

What the result means

If the result is positive, the image still stores GPS coordinates in its metadata. That does not mean the photo visibly shows a map; it means the file keeps the location behind the scenes.

If the result is negative, this tool did not detect readable GPS coordinates in the analyzed file.

Privacy Your photos are processed in your browser. They are not uploaded to our servers.
