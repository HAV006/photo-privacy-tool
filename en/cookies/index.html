Cookies policy

These legal pages explain how this static site works and what it means when processing happens locally in the browser.

Summary

This V1 is not designed to set advertising cookies or upload your photos to a server during normal use of the tools.

Image and metadata processing happen locally in the browser.

Cookies and storage

At the moment, the site does not need first-party functional cookies to inspect EXIF or clean metadata.

Your browser may still use temporary storage or cache to load static files, but that does not mean your photos are sent to our servers.

Third-party services

If analytics, a CDN feature set or external integrations are added later, this policy should be reviewed to reflect any cookie or similar technology involved.

How to manage cookies

You can review, block or remove cookies in your browser settings. Keep in mind that some technical cookies from the hosting or security platform may depend on platform configuration.
