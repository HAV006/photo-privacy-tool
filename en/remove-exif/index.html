Remove EXIF from a photo

Create a new copy of the image without the original EXIF block, directly on your device and without relying on a server.

  • Local browser-side processing.
  • Download a clean copy ready to share.
  • Visible confirmation that the new copy no longer keeps EXIF.

How it works

After you select a photo, the website recreates it locally in the browser to produce a new file that does not keep the EXIF block embedded in the original image.

We then verify the resulting copy to confirm that readable EXIF data is no longer present.

Privacy Your photos are processed in your browser. They are not uploaded to our servers.

Helpful tip

If you only want to check whether the image includes GPS location before cleaning it, use the dedicated location checker first.
